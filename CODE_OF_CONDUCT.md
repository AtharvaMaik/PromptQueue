# Code Of Conduct

PromptQueue should be a useful, low-drama project.

## Expected

- Be respectful and direct.
- Assume good intent, especially from first-time contributors.
- Keep issues and PRs focused on the project.
- Avoid sharing private prompts, secrets, or personal data.

## Not Accepted

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing someone else's private information.
- Repeated off-topic arguing after maintainers ask to move on.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; or block users when needed to keep the project usable.

If you need to report a problem, open a minimal issue asking for a private maintainer contact path. Do not post sensitive details publicly.
